AGGREGATES AND BUILDING MATERIALS



Amasefuhlala Construction also specializes in supply of aggregates used across the construction and architectural design industry. Our Aggregated product range mainly covers the following:

Roadstone


– which is a high quality product with specific grading, shape and wearing properties for asphalt and road surfacing applications.

Concrete Aggregates


– Include a wide range of stone sizes within specific grading as well a washed and unwashed sands for application in all on-site and ready mixed concrete.

Road layer works materials


– Includes the base sub-base materials that provides the sup-porting interlocking structure in road design. The different categories of graded materials cater for varying load designs.

Specialty Aggregates


– Include railway ballast, gabion and other larger sized crushed stones used in erosion protection, architectural and landscape.

We also supply the following building and Construction Materials:
