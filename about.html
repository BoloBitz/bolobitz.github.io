The Company

Mr. Irvin Mohlala is a very dedicated and visionary individual in charge of company. He is the founder and managing director of AMASEFUHLALA CONSTRUCTION AND PROJECTS (PTY) LTD
Amasefuhlala Construction And Projects is a construction and civil engineering company trading under the registered name Amasefuhlala Construction And Projects. Amasefuhlala Construction and Projects is a South African based privately owned company that was established in 2014 with the goal of taking the current flagship in construction and growing it within the Southern Africa region by providing the trust and quality that our customers have learnt to rely on. The company inherently has 100% black managed and owned a status ( BEE ). The primary function of the company is to provide construction services to developers, funding agents, and development organizations.

Our Objective is to provide our clients with assuredness and experience when we are chosen to execute their projects. Our emphasis on clear communication and follow-through procedures ensures that client’s objectives are our top priority in the planning and execution of all our processes.
